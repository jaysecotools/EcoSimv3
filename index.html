Quick Quiz

What happens when devil populations grow too large?

They overhunt pademelons, leading to grass overgrowth and eventual ecosystem collapse.

Why are bandicoots considered a keystone species?

Their digging aerates soil and improves grass growth, benefiting the entire ecosystem.

Did You Know?

Bandicoots are ecosystem engineers - their digging aerates soil and helps grass grow 15-20% faster!

Tasmanian devils have the strongest bite force relative to body size of any living carnivorous mammal.

Ecosystem Facts

Healthy ecosystems maintain balance through predator-prey relationships. When one species declines, it affects all others in the food web.

Biodiversity is a key indicator of ecosystem health. More diverse ecosystems are more resilient to disturbances.
